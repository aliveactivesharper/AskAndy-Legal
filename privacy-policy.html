Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how "Living Active Sharper, LLC" ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with the AskAndy application ("Service"). The Service is powered by the PillarAI platform, which is owned and operated by "Living Active Sharper, LLC." By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Account Registration Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Email Address: Used for account identification, communication, and authentication
  • Full Name: Collected during registration for personalization
  • Password: Stored as a cryptographically hashed value (we cannot view your actual password)
  • Azure AD Information: If you authenticate via Azure Active Directory, we may store your Azure AD Object ID and Tenant ID for account linking

2.2. Authentication and Session Data

To maintain your session and authenticate requests, we collect and store:

  • JWT Tokens: Stored in your browser's local storage for authentication
  • User Session Records: Session tokens, expiration times, and last activity timestamps stored in our database
  • Login Timestamps: Records of when you access the Service

2.3. Conversation and Chat Data

When you use the chat functionality, we automatically collect and store:

  • Your Questions/Messages: All text you submit to the chat interface
  • AI-Generated Responses: All responses generated by the AI system
  • Conversation Metadata: Conversation IDs, titles, timestamps, and message ordering
  • Source Information: References to content sources used to generate responses
  • Follow-up Suggestions: Suggested questions generated by the system
  • Segment IDs: Identifiers for content segments referenced in responses
  • Context Data: Information about content segments used as context for generating responses
  • Feedback Data: Any thumbs up/down feedback you provide on responses
  • Request IDs: Unique identifiers for each chat request for debugging and support

2.4. Email Verification Data

For email verification purposes, we temporarily store:

  • Verification Codes: Six-digit codes sent to your email address
  • Verification Timestamps: When codes were created, expire, and are used

2.5. Content Sharing Data

When you share conversations via email, we collect:

  • Recipient Email Addresses: Email addresses you provide for sharing conversations
  • Shared Content: The conversation content you choose to share
  • Sharing Timestamps: Records of when sharing occurs

Note: We do not collect, store, or retain phone numbers used in SMS sharing features. Phone numbers are used solely to deliver requested messages and are not stored after delivery.

2.6. Video and Document Upload Data

If you upload videos or documents, we collect:

  • File Metadata: File names, sizes, upload dates, and processing status
  • Video Transcripts: Text transcripts extracted from uploaded videos
  • Processing Information: Status of video/document processing and indexing
  • Video/Document Identifiers: Unique IDs assigned to uploaded content

2.7. Content Engagement Data

We track your engagement with content to improve the Service:

  • Video Viewing: Records of which videos you watch
  • Document Viewing: Records of which documents you access
  • Content Interaction: General engagement metrics with various content types

2.8. Technical and Usage Data

We automatically collect technical information including:

  • Application Insights Data: Telemetry data collected through Azure Application Insights for service monitoring, error tracking, and performance analysis
  • Log Analytics Data: System logs and diagnostic information
  • IP Addresses: May be collected for security and fraud prevention
  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and device information
  • Usage Patterns: How you interact with the Service

2.9. Local Storage Data

Your browser may store:

  • Authentication Tokens: Stored in browser local storage for session management
  • User Preferences: Any settings or preferences you configure
  • Session Identifiers: Unique session IDs for conversation tracking

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and manage your account
  • Conversation History: To maintain your conversation history and enable you to access past interactions
  • AI Response Generation: To generate personalized AI responses based on your questions and conversation context
  • Content Sharing: To facilitate sharing of conversations via email or SMS at your request
  • Content Processing: To process, index, and make searchable any videos or documents you upload
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the Service's functionality and accuracy
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address security issues and fraudulent activity
  • Communication: To send verification emails, password reset codes, and service-related communications
  • Support: To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

4. Data Storage and Retention

4.1. Storage Location

Your data is stored in:

  • Azure SQL Database: User accounts, conversations, sessions, and related data
  • Azure Blob Storage: Video transcripts, uploaded files, and related media
  • Azure Video Indexer: Processed video content and metadata
  • Browser Local Storage: Authentication tokens and session data (stored on your device)
  • Azure Application Insights: Telemetry and analytics data

All data is stored in Microsoft Azure data centers, primarily in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer and storage of your data in these locations.

4.2. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

  • Account Data: Retained for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service
  • Conversation Data: Retained indefinitely unless you request deletion or your account is deleted
  • Email Verification Codes: Retained until used or expired (typically 10 minutes)
  • Session Data: Retained for the duration of your session and may be retained for security purposes
  • Video/Document Data: Retained until you delete the content or your account is deleted
  • Application Insights Data: Retained according to Azure Application Insights retention policies (typically 90 days)

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email address provided in Section 12.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

5.2. Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with third-party service providers solely for the purpose of operating the Service:

  • Microsoft Azure Services: We use Azure services (SQL Database, Blob Storage, Video Indexer, Communication Services, Application Insights) to host and operate the Service. These services process your data according to Microsoft's privacy policies and data processing agreements.
  • AI Service Providers: We use Azure AI Foundry and OpenAI services to generate responses. Your questions and conversation context may be processed by these services to generate responses.
  • Email Service Providers: We use Azure Communication Services to send emails. Recipient email addresses are shared with this service solely for message delivery.
  • SMS Service Providers: If you use SMS sharing, phone numbers are shared with telecommunications providers solely for message delivery. We do not retain phone numbers after delivery.

5.3. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others
  • Prevent or investigate fraud or security issues

5.4. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

6. Email and SMS Sharing

6.1. Email Sharing

When you share conversations via email:

  • We collect recipient email addresses you provide
  • We send the shared conversation content to those addresses
  • Recipient email addresses may be logged for delivery confirmation
  • We do not use recipient email addresses for marketing purposes

6.2. SMS Sharing

SMS messages are sent only at the explicit request of the user for the purpose of sharing conversation content with a recipient chosen by the user.

We do not collect, store, or retain phone numbers used in the SMS sharing feature. We do not collect marketing data, behavioral profiles, or analytics related to SMS usage.

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver the requested message and are not stored after delivery.

In cases where a recipient has not previously consented to receive SMS messages, a single message may be sent solely to request consent. No conversation content is transmitted unless the recipient explicitly opts in.

The content shared via SMS originates from conversation history already available to the user within the application. We do not create, modify, or retain new personal data as part of the SMS feature.

Users may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP.

6.3. Your Responsibility

By using sharing features, you confirm that:

  • You have permission to share the selected content
  • You have a relationship with the recipient
  • The recipient reasonably expects to receive the message

The Service does not verify recipient identity and is not responsible for messages sent to incorrect or unintended recipients.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Access Controls: Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel and systems necessary to operate the Service
  • Authentication: We use JWT tokens and secure authentication mechanisms
  • Database Security: Data is stored in secure Azure SQL databases with access controls
  • Regular Updates: We maintain security patches and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • Correction: You can update your account information (email, name) through your account settings
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided in Section 12.

You can clear this data by clearing your browser's local storage, though this will require you to log in again.
