SMS Opt-In Information

Overview

AskAndy provides a user-initiated, transactional SMS sharing feature that allows users to share conversation content with recipients of their choosing.

How SMS Consent Is Obtained

If a recipient has not previously consented to receive SMS messages, the Service will send a single message requesting consent.

Example consent request message:

You are receiving this message because a user of AskAndy would like to share a conversation with you. Reply YES to receive the message. Reply STOP to decline. Message and data rates may apply.

Important: No conversation content is sent unless the recipient replies YES.

Opt-Out and Help

  • Reply STOP at any time to opt out
  • Reply HELP for assistance

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.
